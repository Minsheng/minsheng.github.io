Minsheng is a full stack software developer, from Shenzhen, China. He obtained a bachelor degree in Computer Science from University of Toronto.

He is currently working for Visual Analytics Lab at OCAD University as a full stack developer on a visual exploration and presentation web application. Prior to the graduate program, he worked as a front end developer in Tokyo, Japan for over a year.

Minsheng has always been passionate about video games and virtual reality technologies. His master thesis is about navigation in virtual reality for visual analytics. He had made an autonomous robot, digital fabrication, and even a short film! Outside work, he travels a lot, plays flute, does yoga, and occasionally goes to indoor rock climbing. His motto of the year is 'Don't stop the train!'
