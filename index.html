Davidson is a full stack software developer, from Shenzhen, China. He obtained a bachelor degree in Computer Science from University of Toronto.

He is currently working for Visual Analytics Lab at OCAD University as a full stack developer on a visual exploration and presentation web application. Prior to the graduate program, he worked as a front end developer in Tokyo, Japan for over a year.

Davidson has always been passionate about programming, video gaming, and virtual reality technologies. His current research is about navigation and UI in virtual reality based visualization. He learned to make autonomous robot, digital fabrication, and even a short film! Outside work, he travels a lot, plays flute, does yoga, and occasionally goes to indoor rock climbing. His motto of the year is 'Don't stop the train!.'
